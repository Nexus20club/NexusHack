Online Exam Instructions

Welcome to your online exam. Please read the instructions carefully before starting the test.

General Guidelines

  • You are allowed a limited time to complete the exam.
  • Make sure you have a stable internet connection throughout the exam.
  • Once the exam begins, you will not be able to pause or exit until completed.

Exam Format

  • The exam consists of multiple-choice questions (MCQs) and short answer questions.
  • Each question will have a time limit to answer.
  • Answer all questions to the best of your ability.

Important Notes

  • Do not refresh or navigate away from the exam page during the exam.
  • If you face any technical issues, please contact the exam support team immediately.
